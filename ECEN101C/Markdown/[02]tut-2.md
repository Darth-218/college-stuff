### Question 6

$$P = 60w, \qquad V = 120$$

1.  $$P = V \times I \rightarrow I = \frac{P}{V} = \frac{60}{120} = \frac{1}{2}$$

    - Energy per year $\rightarrow 525.6 \times 9.5 = 4993$
